Changing Rhythm, Stress, and Intonation

DECtalk Software uses stress and syntactic symbols to control aspects of rhythm, stress, and intonation patterns within a spoken text file. These symbols include punctuation marks such as commas, periods, and parentheses. Punctuation marks are recognized by DECtalk Software as indicating special phrasing requirements.
