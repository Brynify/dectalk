Monaural Volume Control

The following monaural volume commands are supported. DD must be in the range of 0 to 99.

SYNTAX:
[:volume XX DD]
ABBREVIATION:
[:volu XX DD]
ALTERNATE COMMAND:
none
OPTIONS:
set
Sets the volume to the desired level

up
Increases the volume by the desired amount

down
Decreases the volume by the desired amount
PARAMETERS:
Volume amount
DEFAULT:
none


EXAMPLES:
[:volume up 30]
