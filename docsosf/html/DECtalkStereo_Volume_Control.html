Stereo Volume Control

The following stereo volume commands are supported. DD1 or DD2 must be in the range of 0 to 99.

SYNTAX:
[:volume XX DD1 DD2]
ABBREVIATION:
[:volu XX DD1 DD2]]
ALTERNATE COMMAND:
[:volume XX DD]
OPTIONS:
lset
Sets the left channel to the desired level

lup
Increases the left channel by the desired amount

ldown
Decreases the left channel by the desired amount

rset
Sets the right channel to the desired level

rup
Increases the right channel by the desired amount

rdown
Decreases the right channel by the desired amount

sset
Sets the left channel to the DD1 amount and the right channel to the DD2 amount
PARAMETERS:
Volume amount(s)
DEFAULT:
none


EXAMPLES:
[:volume sset 80 60]
